Top Experiences

Brixton Village and Market Row

A world-famous market packed with independent boutiques, clothing stores, and food stalls serving global cuisines.

Visit Official Site

Electric Avenue

One of London's first market streets to get electric streetlighting, now famous for its bustling market stalls and energy.

Travel Information

Take the London Underground via the Victoria Line straight to Brixton Station. There are also many local bus routes.

The markets run daily from 8:00 AM until late evening, with weekends being the busiest and most vibrant days.
